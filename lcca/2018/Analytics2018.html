Help

Event
A race event (Mass Start or Time Trial). An Event could have single or multiple categories.
Competition
A single or multi-day collection of Events with the same organizer.
License Holder
A License Holder cooresponds to a unique person (holds a license). A License Holder can attend a Competition as an Attendee, and race in any number of Events at the Competition as a Participant.
Category
The Category of the License Holder in the Event. License Holder may compete in different Events in different Categories at the same Competition.
Attendee
A License Holder attending a Competition. If the License Holder participates in more than one Events, in the same or in different categories, he/she is only counted once at the Competition. Unless otherwise specified, counts in the Analytics pages are shown by Attendees.
Participant
An Attendee who starts in a race with a Bib number. The same Attendee can be a Participant more then once at the same Competition if he/she races in multiple Events (either in the same category or in different categories). The number of Participants at a Competition is always greater or equal to the number of Attendees. This metric helps understand the work on the race administration.
